    Screen Shader helps you control your circadian rythm! 😎

    Feeling tired? Screen Shader will ease you into sleep automatically. The increased shade will increase melatonin hormone production right before sleep. So be sure to set what time you wake up!

    Ready to work? Screen Shader keeps you awake during the daytime. As long as the sun is up where you live your screen stays bright! During the night it will turn down the brightness since the sun isn't up anymore.

    Pick a color that lowers your exposure to blue light and is comfortable on your eyes. These colors are warm, redish, or orangish colors. The first 6 color choices are great for most users!

    However you may want to choose another color. Sufferers of macular edema might benefit from using a blue shade color to increase contrast between colors. So the choice totally depends on you!

    Give Screen Shader a location and it will do the rest! 😀

    Screen Shader's algorithm changes the shade according to local sunset and sunrise times to best match up with your biological clock. 🌞
